TAIWANESE MAHJONG

Taiwanese Mahjong is a fast-paced, social tile game played by four players that combines strategy, memory, and a bit of luck. Each player starts with 16 tiles and takes turns drawing and discarding to form a winning hand made of sets and a pair. Learn the tiles, melds, scoring, and test your knowledge!

~10 minutes | No prior experience required
